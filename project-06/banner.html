Space exploration has always been one of humanity's greatest achievements. From the first astronauts walking on the Moon to living and working aboard the International Space Station (ISS), humans have continuously pushed the boundaries of what is possible. These missions not only expand our understanding of the universe but also lead to important discoveries that benefit life on Earth.
